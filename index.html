About Karma '26

Karma, the crowning jewel in our annual calendar, represents more than just an event; it stands as the epitome of innovation, collaboration, and cultural richness at KMCT College of Engineering. This Annual National Level Techno-Socio-Cultural extravaganza is a grand celebration that goes beyond the ordinary.

At Karma, we create an immersive experience that seamlessly integrates technology, socio-cultural vibrancy, and academic excellence. Join us in this grand celebration, where each moment is a testament to the limitless potential within the KMCT College of Engineering community.
